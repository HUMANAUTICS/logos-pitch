The Core Problem

Reactive Workflows & Continuity Risk

Document Archive Vulnerability

Business owners rarely maintain organized document archives, relying on CPA portals for storage. When a Tax Pro retires, closes their firm, or passes away, portal access windows quickly close—leaving owners reliant on surviving partners, staff, or family to recover vital records.

The Heavy Lift of Changing Tax Pros

Gathering years of unorganized historical filings and onboarding a new tax pro requires significant effort. Even when unhappy with service, business owners default to staying put because changing providers feels like an overwhelming lift.

"Tight IRS deadlines and last-minute client document collection force the industry into a reactive state. Combined with unorganized archives, business owners default to staying put because changing tax pros feels like an overwhelming lift."

Logos eliminates the heavy lift of switching tax pros and guarantees permanent document continuity—turning reactive tax prep into proactive year-round strategy.

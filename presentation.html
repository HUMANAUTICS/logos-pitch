The Core Problem

Scattered Data & The Black Box Trap

Data Monopolization

Your entire strategic tax planning, documentation history, and compliance files live inside your CPA's proprietary internal systems. You cannot access it or own it.

The "Zero-Start" Trap

When you switch accountants, you start from scratch. The new CPA has no historical context, leading to double onboarding charges, duplicate audits, and missed deductions.

"Traditional accounting relationships rely on keeping business owners in the dark. By locking context in proprietary client management systems, switching CPAs becomes a painful process."

The average high-growth startup switches accountants every 2.4 years due to poor service or high fees—paying thousands to start over.

The Logos Solution

Flipping the Power Dynamics of Tax Data

We have built the first **portable database** for corporate tax. The business owner retains full custody of their financial records, AI advisory logs, and strategy implementation steps.

Permanent Sovereignty

Your tax details belong to you. When you switch CPAs, you simply revoke their database credentials and hand the same dashboard and historical pipeline to your next CPA.
